Role-Playing Prompts
Act as a [SPECIFIC ROLE] with [X years] of experience in [FIELD]. Your expertise includes [KEY SKILLS/KNOWLEDGE AREAS]. When responding, always consider [SPECIFIC PERSPECTIVE/CONSTRAINTS] and provide insights that a [ROLE] would typically offer.

Step-by-Step Frameworks
Break down [COMPLEX TASK] into clear, actionable steps: 1. Analysis: First, examine [KEY COMPONENTS] 2. Strategy: Then, develop [APPROACH/METHOD] 3. Implementation: Next, execute [SPECIFIC ACTIONS] 4. Validation: Finally, verify [SUCCESS CRITERIA] For each step, provide specific examples and potential challenges.

⚡ Advanced Strategies

🎭
Chain-of-Thought Prompting
Let's work through this step-by-step: First, I need to understand: [STATE THE PROBLEM] Then, I should consider: [LIST KEY FACTORS] Next, I'll analyze: [BREAK DOWN COMPONENTS] Finally, I'll conclude: [SYNTHESIZE SOLUTION] Walk me through your reasoning process for each step.
🔄
Iterative Refinement
Version 1: Provide an initial [SOLUTION/RESPONSE] to [PROBLEM] Version 2: Now improve it by [SPECIFIC ENHANCEMENT CRITERIA] Version 3: Make it even better by addressing [ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS] Final Version: Polish it considering [QUALITY STANDARDS] and [TARGET AUDIENCE] Show me each iteration with explanations for improvements.
⚖️
Multi-Perspective Analysis
Analyze [TOPIC/SITUATION] from multiple perspectives: 🔵 Perspective 1 - [STAKEHOLDER A]: [Their viewpoint and concerns] 🟡 Perspective 2 - [STAKEHOLDER B]: [Their viewpoint and concerns] 🟢 Perspective 3 - [STAKEHOLDER C]: [Their viewpoint and concerns] Then synthesize these perspectives to find: - Common ground - Key conflicts - Balanced solutions

Constraint-Based Prompting
Create [OUTPUT TYPE] with these specific constraints: ✅ Must include: [REQUIRED ELEMENTS] ❌ Must avoid: [PROHIBITED ELEMENTS] 📏 Length: [SPECIFIC WORD/CHARACTER COUNT] 🎯 Tone: [SPECIFIC TONE/STYLE] 👥 Audience: [TARGET DEMOGRAPHIC] 📅 Timeline: [DELIVERY/CONTEXT TIMEFRAME] Optimize for [PRIMARY GOAL] while maintaining [QUALITY STANDARDS].
🧩
Template Generation
Create a reusable template for [SPECIFIC USE CASE] that includes: 📋 Structure: [DEFINE SECTIONS/COMPONENTS] 🔧 Variables: {PLACEHOLDER_FIELDS} for customization 📝 Instructions: Clear guidance for each section ✨ Examples: Sample content for reference 🎯 Success criteria: How to evaluate results Make it adaptable for [DIFFERENT SCENARIOS/CONTEXTS].
